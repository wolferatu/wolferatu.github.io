PORTAL COMA

Currently in development

Delve into a terrifying underground facility where a mysterious portal machine is waiting.

Portal Coma is an atmospheric first-person horror game with stealth elements about escaping things that came out of a portal. You’ll delve into a terrifying underground facility where a mysterious portal machine is used to exchange items with something on the other side. Stealth will be needed to survive and you will need make meaningful choices in order to escape and learn the secret inside.
